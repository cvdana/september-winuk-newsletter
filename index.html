HELLO WINUK COMMUNITY,

As summer comes to an end and we go back to our routines, September reminds us of what we have left on pause and motivates us to strive for the better.
In September, we raise particular awareness on Alzheimer’s disease and challenge the stigma around dementia. Memory loss is not part of normal ageing and a diagnosis of dementia profoundly affects the patient and their loved ones. However, even if a memory could have a short retention for affected patients, the feelings associated with it remain deeply imprinted. At WiNUK we support research on dementia and Alzheimer through events, blogs and social advertisements aimed at raising awareness and facilitating collaborative research in the field.

SOCIALS RECAP

Pain Awareness Month

Pain Awareness Month takes place every September to raise awareness about issues related to chronic pain and pain management.

It aims to educate the public and healthcare professionals about the challenges faced by people living with chronic pain and the importance of finding effective treatments and support.

It's an opportunity to advocate for better understanding, empathy, and improved access to pain management resources for those affected.

WiNUK content creators and graphics team have worked hard to make content to spread more awareness - covering sex differences in pain, recommended reading around the subject, and our very video content creator Neave showcasing her experience attending the biggest pain conference, bringing together more than 5,000 scientists, clinicians, and healthcare providers from around the world 🌍
